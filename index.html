An overview of my professional life.

My current career goals are oriented towards becoming an engineer who implements DevOps concepts and technologies to extend the Agile or Lean methodology. I enjoy learning new technologies and am fascinated by the ability to orchestrate OS level system virtualization for the creation and deployment of products.
I am a leader by nurture, learning how to lead by being a follower. I pride myself to be able to strike up a conversation with anyone.
I am a curious person and enjoy talking and learning from other individuals. I also enjoy teaching concepts especially those which requires intuition, it gives me immense joy to see someone connect the dots and go "Wait a minute...".

    What I bring to the table:
  • Proven ability to lead and solve challenges
  • Theoretical understanding of DevOps concepts
  • Comfortable with Asnible, Puppet, Chef, Docker and Kubernetes
  • Intuitive understanding of Linux Administrative concepts
  • Proficiency in Python and comfortable with Java, C++, C, Bash, CSS, HTML, Javascript, React, YAML and Lua
  • Strong command line skills for working within virtualized machines (bash, vim)
  • Experience with distributed version control like Git
  • Comfortable with database like MySQL
  • A proven ability to build rapport with individuals from all backgrounds
